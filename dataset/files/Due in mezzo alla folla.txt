Larry Stevens is about to be evicted by landlady Lillie for not paying his rent. He happens to be passing by, as does Julia Wayne, when two halves of a ripped $1,000 bill float down to the street.
Lassù, il gangster Bonelli ne ha distribuiti migliaia alle sue ragazze. Uno che è arrabbiato con lui lo ha strappato e gettato dalla finestra.
Skeeter, a jockey, joins up with Julia and Larry as they discuss what to do with the money. Julia ha un debito di 500 dollari da ripagare. Larry wants to use it to enter his horse Hector's Pal in a big race.
The money was stolen from a bank where Larry takes the torn $1,000 bill. Un detective sospetto, Flynn, inizia a seguire Larry, che attira anche l'attenzione dell'attore disoccupato Anthony e del cassiere di banca Bennett, che vogliono prendere parte all'azione.
Larry is in love with Julia and wants to help fulfill her dream of performing in a show. A theatrical producer pretends to hire her on talent, but secretly has schemed with Larry to finance the show if his horse wins the race. Julia races to the race track to see how it all turns out.
